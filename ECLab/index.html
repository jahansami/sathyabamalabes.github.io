Experiment

Hours Of lab class

Experiments

Understand the fundamental principles of circuit analysis, including Kirchhoff's laws, Ohm's law, and basic circuit theorems, such as the voltage divider rule and current divider rule.

Develop skills in designing and building electronic circuits, including the ability to select appropriate components, use test equipment, and troubleshoot circuits.

Learn about the properties and applications of common electronic components, such as resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors, and operational amplifiers, and how they can be used to build various types of circuits, such as amplifiers, filters, and oscillators.

Experiment 1

TRANSISTOR BIASING CIRCUITS

The process of determining a transistors DC operating voltage or current conditions (i.e, Q-point) and to observe the node values.

Experiment 2

HALFWAVE RECTIFIER (HWR)

To construct a half-wave bridge rectifier circuit with and without filter and analyze its output.

Experiment 3

VOLTAGE REGULATOR

To study Zener diode as voltage regulator: 1. To calculate % line regulation 2. To calculate % load regulation

Experiment 4

FULLWAVE RECTIFIER (FWR)

To construct a full-wave bridge rectifier circuit with and without filter and analyze its output.

Experiment 5

FLIPFLOPS

To realize and implement SR, JK, D and T Flip-Flops using Multisim and verifying it with truth table.

Experiment 6

ADDER

To design a combinational circuit that performs addition of both two bits and three bits and hence verifying with the truth table.

Experiment 7

SUBRTRACTOR

To design a combinational circuit that performs subtraction of both two bits and three bits and hence verifying with the truth table.

Experiment 8

SHIFT REGISTER

To design and realize and study of Shift Register. 1: SISO (Serial in Serial out) 2: SIPO (Serial in Parallel out) 3: PIPO (Parallel in Parallel out) 4: PISO (Parallel in Serial out)

Experiment 9

COUNTER

To realize a 3-bit counter as a sequential circuit.

Experiment 10

CODE CONVERTERS

To convert 4-bit Binary numbers into Gray code and verifying with the truth table.

Experiment 11

COMMON SOURCE JFET

To study the JFET common source amplifier and find it’s cut off frequencies and Bandwidth.

Experiment 12

BJT COMMON EMITTER AMPLIFIER

To study the BJT Common Emitter Amplifier and find it’s cut off frequencies and Bandwidth.
